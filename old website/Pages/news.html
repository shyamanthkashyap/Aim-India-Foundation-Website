Community Cleaning - Clean India Drive

March 20, 2017

As a regular activity the Trustees and volunteers of the Foundation starteted community cleaning in slum area of South Kolkata at 8.30 AM by cleaning the drains and the curbs of the locality creating very positive response from the residents. They spray bleaching powder along the drain and demonstrated to the residents the good effect of such cleaning; when the residents experienced the reduction and elimination of off-odour around the vicinity. Umbrella Distribution Camp as an Educational Initiative

August 7, 2016

There are children among our community, who do not attend schools during raining season, not because they do not want to attend, but because they do not have umbrella to protect them from lashing rain and consequent sickness. The Foundation has decided to make a beginning by free distribution of umbrellas to those needy school-going children and to build this into a campaign.

The Foundation has initiated this need-based program by distributing over 341 umbrellas to the needy school children of slum areas at Panchanantala Shishu Prathamik Vidyapith, 32 Panchanantala Road, Kolkata- 700029 on the 7th of August 2016 at 10:30 AM. Read More...

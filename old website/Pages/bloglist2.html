PLANT A TREE TO BE DANGER FREE - A total discussion - Ashwarya Ralhan

Tree plantation is the process of planting seedlings, generally for forestry and land reclamation. We cannot live without oxygen, even for a minute. Without trees, the animal world will die away. Carbon dioxide makes our atmosphere poisonous for us, and nowadays, we are surrounded by carbon dioxide in huge amounts. Trees make our environment safe for us. They also give us many other benefits. We get fruits and flowers from the trees, they provide us fuel and timbers and also protects us from rays of sun. They check the quick flow of rainwater and thereby check the occurrence of high flood in our rivers. Trees are very important part of our life.

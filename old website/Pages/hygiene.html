Our Clean India Drive

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a cleanliness drive aimed to cover all over India in order to clean the streets, roads and other infrastructure. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a cleanliness campaign run by the government of India and initiated by the Honourable Prime Minister.

It is a national level campaign run by the Indian Government to cover all the backward statutory towns to make them clean. This campaign involves the construction of latrines, promoting sanitation programmes in the rural areas, cleaning streets, roads and changing the infrastructure of the country to lead the country ahead.

It is a politics free campaign and inspired by the patriotism. It is launched as a responsibility of the each and every Indian citizen to make this country a Swachh country. This campaign has initiated people globally towards the cleanliness. Volunteers of Aim India Foundation are joining this “Clean India Campaign” very actively with great fervour and joy.

What was the need of this campaign?

To make the country cleaner the campaign was launched by Government of India. The campaign must go in the country till it does not get its goal. It is very important to keep running this campaign until the whole India comes in real means which can be started by gathering all over the cleanliness.

Below are the few Important topics which must be considered

1. It was the dream of Mahatma Gandhi to make the country clean and we must fulfil it.

2. It is necessary to educate the rural population about the sustainable sanitation practice by motivating them and organising health education.

3. It is necessary to improve the standard of life and its quality of the rural areas of the country.

4. Participation of private sectors and nonprofit organisations in the development of sanitation facility is important.

A pollution free clean India would be the best tribute to the nation. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a cleanliness mass movement organized by the government of India and takes into action by the honourable Prime Minister Mr. Narender Modi. This is the most valuable expedition which everyone must be aware of for the bright future of India.

Together you can make the change. An Eco-friendly society is always needed its citizen to be healthy and clean in every face of life.
